About EV Brightline

Powering the Future of Electric Mobility

EV Brightline is an intelligent EV charging management platform designed to connect drivers, charging station owners, and the power grid into one seamless ecosystem. The solution simplifies the charging experience for electric vehicle (EV) drivers while empowering charging station owners with powerful analytics, real-time monitoring, and business insights.

Our mission is to accelerate sustainable transportation by offering scalable, secure, and intuitive digital solutions that make EV charging smarter and more accessible.

Maturity & Adoption Widely adopted, stable, proven Latest version, growing adoption 1.6 ensures broad compatibility; 2.0.1 is future‑ready for modern networks

Device & Network Management Basic remote control Comprehensive device lifecycle and diagnostics Reduces manual maintenance; supports scaling thousands of chargers

Integration & Extension Simple cloud integration Modern APIs and event model for seamless SaaS connectivity Easier to integrate with ERP, CRM, and EMS platforms
